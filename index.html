Meet Our Bunnies

Welcome to Bunnienest — a small-scale Holland Lop rabbitry nestled in Hemet, California. We pour love into every litter, handling our babies daily from birth so they grow up social, confident, and ready for their forever homes.

Each bunny is raised with individual attention, proper nutrition, and plenty of snuggle time. We believe that happy babies make happy pets, and we take pride in sending home bunnies that are healthy, well-socialized, and full of personality.
